Detailed Reviews

1. Runway ML Gen-3

Runway ML has evolved from a research project into a full-fledged AI video editing suite. Gen-3 brings real-time video generation, motion tracking, and background removal that feels like magic.

Key Features:

  • Text-to-video generation (up to 1080p)
  • Inpainting and outpainting
  • Green screen removal with no keying
  • Multi-track timeline with AI-assisted editing

Pricing: Free tier (720p, 5 projects), Standard $12/month, Pro $28/month, Unlimited $76/month. No enterprise plan listed.

Pros: Incredible AI generation quality, intuitive interface, fast rendering.

Cons: Free tier is limited, some features require high-end GPU.

2. Descript

Descript is the go-to for text-based editing. You edit video by editing a transcript—every cut, trim, or delete happens automatically. It’s a game-changer for YouTubers and podcasters.

Key Features:

  • Text-based editing with automatic filler word removal
  • AI voice cloning (Studio Sound)
  • Screen recording and caption generation
  • Export to all major platforms

Pricing: Free (1 hour export), Hobbyist $24/month, Pro $40/month. Enterprise custom.

Pros: Saves hours on editing, excellent for spoken-word content, collaboration features.

Cons: Limited visual effects, not ideal for complex multi-cam projects.

3. Adobe Premiere Pro (AI Features)

Adobe’s Sensei AI is baked into Premiere Pro, offering auto-reframe, scene edit detection, and text-based editing (beta). It’s still the industry standard for a reason.

Key Features:

  • Auto-reframe for social media
  • Speech-to-text and auto-captions
  • Color matching and audio auto-ducking
  • Integration with After Effects and Audition

Pricing: $22.99/month (single app), $54.99/month (Creative Cloud all apps). No free tier.

Pros: Unmatched professional features, huge community, plugin ecosystem.

Cons: Steep learning curve, subscription-only, AI features are still being rolled out.

4. Pictory

Pictory excels at repurposing long-form content (webinars, podcasts) into short, shareable clips. It uses AI to find the best moments.

Key Features:

  • Automatic highlight extraction
  • Text-to-video from scripts
  • Auto-captioning and brand templates

Pricing: Starter $19/month, Professional $39/month, Team $99/month. No free tier.

Pros: Great for repurposing, easy to use, good for marketing teams.

Cons: Limited manual editing, output quality can be inconsistent.

5. InVideo AI

InVideo AI is designed for quick social media videos. Just type a prompt, and it generates a full video with stock footage, transitions, and voiceover.

Key Features:

  • AI script and video generation
  • 1,000+ templates
  • Auto voiceover in multiple languages

Pricing: Free (watermarked), Plus $20/month, Max $60/month.

Pros: Super fast, no editing skills needed, good for beginners.

Cons: Watermark on free plan, limited customization, output can feel generic.

6. Kapwing

Kapwing is a browser-based editor with strong collaboration features. Its AI tools include background removal, auto-captions, and smart crop.

Key Features:

  • Real-time team collaboration
  • AI-powered subtitles and translation
  • Smart resize for different platforms

Pricing: Free (720p, 4-min videos), Plus $16/month (billed yearly), Pro $50/month. Enterprise custom.

Pros: Great for teams, no software install, generous free tier.

Cons: Export limits on free plan, advanced AI features are behind paywall.

FAQ

What is the best AI video editing tool for beginners in 2025?

InVideo AI and Kapwing are the most beginner-friendly. InVideo AI generates videos from text prompts, while Kapwing offers a simple browser interface with AI subtitles and background removal.

Can AI video editing tools replace human editors?

Not entirely. AI excels at repetitive tasks (cutting, captioning, color matching) but lacks creative intuition. Professional editors use AI to speed up workflows, not replace human judgment.

Are there any free AI video editing tools without watermarks?

Yes. Runway ML’s free tier has no watermark but limits exports to 720p. Descript’s free plan also has no watermark but caps export time to 1 hour per month.

Which AI video editor is best for long-form to short-form repurposing?

Pictory is specifically designed for this. It automatically detects highlights and creates short clips from webinars, podcasts, or long videos.

Does Adobe Premiere Pro have AI features in 2025?

Yes. Adobe Sensei powers auto-reframe, speech-to-text, scene edit detection, and audio auto-ducking. More AI features are being added regularly.

What is the price of Runway ML in 2025?

Runway ML offers a free tier (720p, 5 projects), Standard $12/month, Pro $28/month, and Unlimited $76/month. Enterprise plans are custom.

Can AI video editing tools generate videos from scratch?

Yes. Runway ML Gen-3 and InVideo AI can generate full videos from text prompts. However, quality varies and often needs manual tweaking.
