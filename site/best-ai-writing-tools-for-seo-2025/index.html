Detailed Reviews of Each Tool

1. Jasper – Best for Creative, Long-Form Content

Jasper (formerly Jarvis) is one of the most popular AI writing tools, known for its high-quality output and brand voice customization. It’s ideal for blog posts, articles, and marketing copy where tone and creativity matter.

Key Features:

  • Long-form assistant for drafting 1,500+ word articles
  • Brand voice profiles to maintain consistency
  • Integration with Surfer SEO for on-page optimization
  • 50+ templates for different content types
  • Plagiarism checker (paid add-on)

Pricing: Creator plan starts at $49/month (unlimited words). Pro plan at $69/month adds more features. No free tier, but a 7-day free trial is available.

Pros: Excellent content quality; robust brand voice control; seamless Surfer integration.

Cons: No built-in SEO scoring; higher price point; can require editing for factual accuracy.

2. Writesonic – Best Budget All-in-One

Writesonic offers a great balance of price and performance, with built-in SEO tools that help you optimize content directly. It’s perfect for small businesses and freelancers.

Key Features:

  • AI article writer with SEO mode (includes keyword suggestions, readability score)
  • Chatsonic – a ChatGPT-like assistant for brainstorming
  • Built-in fact-checking and brand voice
  • Integration with WordPress and other CMS

Pricing: Free tier (10,000 words/month). Starter plan at $20/month (100,000 words). Professional plan at $27/month. Custom enterprise pricing available.

Pros: Affordable; includes basic SEO features; good for both short and long content.

Cons: Output can be inconsistent on complex topics; limited customization compared to Jasper.

3. Rytr – Best for Quick Short-Form Content

Rytr is a low-cost tool for generating social media posts, emails, and short blog sections. It’s not ideal for full-length articles but excels at speed and simplicity.

Key Features:

  • 40+ use-case templates
  • Built-in plagiarism checker
  • Multiple tone options
  • Browser extension

Pricing: Free plan (10,000 characters/month). Saver plan at $9/month (50,000 characters). Unlimited plan at $29/month.

Pros: Very cheap; easy to use; good for short copy.

Cons: Limited for SEO articles; no long-form capabilities; character-based limits are restrictive.

4. Copy.ai – Best for Marketing Copy

Copy.ai focuses on marketing and sales copy, with a strong workflow for creating ad copy, email sequences, and landing pages. Its SEO features are lighter but improving.

Key Features:

  • Infobase for brand knowledge
  • Workflow automation for content series
  • Integration with Google Docs and Zapier
  • Blog post wizard

Pricing: Free plan (2,000 words/month). Starter plan at $36/month (unlimited words). Growth plan at $186/month.

Pros: Excellent for marketing funnels; strong collaboration features; unlimited words on paid plans.

Cons: SEO features are basic; not ideal for long-form ranking content; expensive for advanced plans.

5. Surfer SEO – Best for Data-Driven Optimization

Surfer SEO is not a writing tool per se, but it integrates with AI writers (like Jasper) to provide real-time content optimization suggestions based on top-ranking pages. Essential for serious SEO.

Key Features:

  • Content editor with NLP keyword recommendations
  • Audit tool for existing pages
  • Grow flow for content planning
  • Integration with Google Docs, WordPress, and Jasper

Pricing: No free tier. Starter plan at $69/month (limited audits). Pro plan at $149/month. Business plan at $249/month.

Pros: Data-backed recommendations; improves ranking potential; works with other tools.

Cons: No content generation; steep learning curve; expensive for small sites.

6. Frase.io – Best for Content Research

Frase helps you research and outline content by analyzing SERP data. It can also generate AI content, but its strength lies in brief creation and optimization.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered content briefs
  • Topic clustering and content strategy
  • Integration with Google Search Console
  • AI writer (limited words per plan)

Pricing: Free trial available. Starter plan at $15/month (limited AI writing). Basic plan at $45/month. Team plan at $85/month.

Pros: Great for research; affordable entry point; useful for content teams.

Cons: AI writing quality is average; word limits on lower plans; not a standalone writer.

Content strategists: Frase.io is ideal for research and briefs, especially if you outsource writing to humans or other AI tools.

Enterprise teams: Look at Jasper’s enterprise plan or a custom setup with Surfer SEO for full control and scalability.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can AI writing tools really help with SEO rankings?

Yes, when used correctly. AI tools can help you create content faster and implement on-page SEO best practices like keyword density, heading structure, and readability. However, Google still rewards authority, originality, and user experience—so human oversight is essential.

2. Do I need a separate SEO tool with an AI writer?

Not necessarily. Some AI writers like Writesonic include basic SEO features. But for deep optimization, pairing a writer like Jasper with Surfer SEO or Frase.io gives you a competitive edge.

3. Which AI writing tool is best for long-form content?

Jasper is widely considered the best for long-form content due to its long-form assistant and brand voice control. Writesonic also performs well for articles over 1,500 words.

4. Are there any free AI writing tools good for SEO?

Writesonic and Rytr offer free tiers with limited words. They are good for testing but not sufficient for serious SEO content production. Frase.io has a free trial.

5. Can I use these tools for guest posting or client work?

Absolutely. Most tools allow commercial use. Always check the terms—especially for enterprise plans—and ensure you edit content to avoid duplication and improve quality.

6. How much does a good AI writing tool cost per month?

You can get started for as low as $9/month (Rytr) for basic needs. For full SEO content creation, budget around $20–$70/month for a single tool, or up to $150/month for a premium combo.

7. What is the best AI SEO writing tool for beginners?

Writesonic is beginner-friendly with its intuitive interface and built-in SEO mode. Rytr is also easy but limited for long-form content.
