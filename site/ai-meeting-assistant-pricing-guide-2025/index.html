Detailed Reviews of Each Tool

1. Otter.ai – The Enterprise Powerhouse

Otter.ai has been a leader in AI transcription for years, and its 2025 updates make it even more robust. It offers real-time transcription, automated slide capture, and integration with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. The AI-generated summaries are surprisingly accurate, and the searchable transcript library is a lifesaver for research teams.

Pricing Breakdown:

  • Free: 300 minutes/month, 30 min per conversation
  • Pro: $16.99/month (billed annually) – 1,200 minutes/month, advanced search, export
  • Business: $30/user/month – unlimited transcripts, admin controls, Salesforce integration
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing – SSO, dedicated support, custom models

Pros: Highly accurate transcription, excellent for long meetings, robust enterprise features.

Cons: Free tier is quite limited, interface can feel cluttered, no video recording.

2. Fireflies.ai – Best Value for Teams

Fireflies.ai is our top pick because it balances powerful features with affordable pricing. It automatically joins your meetings (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet), transcribes them, and creates searchable notes. The standout feature is its integration with CRM and project management tools—you can push action items directly to Asana or Salesforce.

Pricing Breakdown:

  • Free: 3 seats, 800 minutes/month, 7-day history
  • Pro: $10/month (billed annually) – unlimited transcripts, AI summaries, 30-day history
  • Business: $19/user/month – advanced analytics, priority support, unlimited history
  • Enterprise: Custom – dedicated server, custom integrations

Pros: Generous free tier, excellent Slack integration, AI-generated action items.

Cons: Transcription accuracy can dip with heavy accents, no native desktop app.

3. Fathom – The Simple, Free Option

Fathom positions itself as the no-fuss meeting assistant. It’s incredibly easy to set up—just install the browser extension and it records, transcribes, and summarizes your meetings automatically. The free version is surprisingly capable, but paid plans unlock more storage and features.

Pricing Breakdown:

  • Free: Unlimited recordings, 5 hours of transcription/month, 1 workspace
  • Pro: $29/month – unlimited transcription, custom templates, priority support
  • Team: $49/user/month – admin controls, team analytics, SSO

Pros: Dead simple to use, generous free tier, clean interface.

Cons: Limited integrations, no real-time collaboration, no mobile app.

4. Grain – For Sales Teams Who Love Video Clips

Grain is built with sales teams in mind. It not only transcribes meetings but also lets you create highlight reels—short video clips of key moments. This is gold for onboarding, training, and sharing customer insights across the org.

Pricing Breakdown:

  • Starter: $15/month (billed annually) – 20 recordings/month, 10 clips, 1 user
  • Business: $39/user/month – unlimited recordings, advanced search, CRM integration
  • Enterprise: Custom – dedicated support, custom branding, API access

Pros: Unique video clipping feature, excellent for sales enablement, easy sharing.

Cons: No free tier, expensive per user, not ideal for non-sales teams.

5. Airgram – The All-in-One Recorder

Airgram is a solid all-rounder that offers recording, transcription, and note-taking in one package. It integrates with most major meeting platforms and provides a searchable transcript library. The AI summaries are decent, but not as polished as Otter or Fireflies.

Pricing Breakdown:

  • Free: 5 meetings/month, 30 min per meeting
  • Pro: $18/month (billed annually) – 30 meetings/month, AI summaries, custom templates
  • Business: $36/user/month – unlimited meetings, admin controls, priority support

Pros: Good all-around features, easy to use, strong privacy controls.

Cons: Free tier is very restrictive, AI summaries can be hit-or-miss, no video clipping.

Who Should Use What

Freelancers & Solopreneurs

If you’re a one-person show, Fathom is your best bet. Its free tier covers most needs, and the setup is painless. For those who need more transcription minutes, Fireflies.ai is a worthy upgrade at just $10/month.

Small to Medium Teams (2-20 people)

Fireflies.ai shines here. The Pro plan at $10/user/month is hard to beat, especially with Slack and CRM integrations. If your team relies heavily on video clips for training or sales, consider Grain.

Enterprise & Large Organizations

Otter.ai is the clear winner for enterprises that need robust admin controls, SSO, and custom models. The Business plan at $30/user/month is competitive given the advanced analytics and search capabilities.

Sales Teams

Grain is tailor-made for sales. The video clipping feature is unmatched for sharing call highlights with prospects or internal teams. However, if you already use Salesforce heavily, Fireflies.ai offers deeper CRM integration at a lower price.

Bottom Line Verdict

Choosing the right AI meeting assistant comes down to your team size, budget, and specific needs. Fireflies.ai offers the best balance of features and affordability for most teams. Otter.ai is the enterprise standard. Fathom is perfect for freelancers who want a free, simple solution. And Grain is a niche tool for sales teams that value video.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest AI meeting assistant?

Fathom offers a generous free tier that includes unlimited recordings and 5 hours of transcription per month. For paid plans, Fireflies.ai starts at $10/month, making it the most affordable premium option.

Which AI meeting assistant has the best free tier?

Fireflies.ai offers the best free tier with 800 minutes of transcription per month and support for up to 3 users. Fathom also has a strong free tier with unlimited recordings but caps transcription at 5 hours/month.

Do AI meeting assistants work with Microsoft Teams?

Yes, most major tools integrate with Microsoft Teams. Otter.ai, Fireflies.ai, and Airgram all support Teams. Fathom and Grain currently only support Zoom and Google Meet.

Are AI meeting assistants accurate?

Accuracy varies by tool and audio quality. Otter.ai and Fireflies.ai consistently score above 90% accuracy in clear recordings. Heavy accents, background noise, or overlapping speech can reduce accuracy. Most tools allow you to edit transcripts manually.

Can I use an AI meeting assistant for free forever?

Yes, many tools offer permanent free tiers with limitations. Fathom and Fireflies.ai both have free plans that don’t expire. However, free tiers often cap storage, transcription minutes, or user seats.

Which AI meeting assistant is best for sales teams?

Grain is specifically designed for sales teams with its video clipping feature. Fireflies.ai is also excellent due to its deep CRM integrations and action item tracking.

Do these tools record video as well as audio?

Most tools record audio and generate text transcripts. Grain records full video, while Otter.ai and Fireflies.ai capture slides and screen shares but not full video. Fathom and Airgram record audio only.
