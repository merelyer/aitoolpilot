Detailed Review of Each Option

1. Descript – Best All-Rounder

Descript is like a word processor for video. You edit the transcript, and the video edits itself. It's incredibly intuitive for beginners and powerful enough for pros.

Pricing:

  • Free: 1 hour of transcription, limited exports
  • Hobbyist: $12/month – 5 hours transcription, watermark-free
  • Pro: $24/month – Unlimited transcription, AI voice cloning, screen recording
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing

Pros:

  • Super easy to use, even for non-editors
  • AI voice cloning (Studio Sound) is top-notch
  • Collaboration features work seamlessly

Cons:

  • Multi-track editing is limited compared to Premiere Pro
  • Free tier is quite restrictive

2. Runway ML – Powerhouse for Professionals

Runway ML is the go-to for advanced AI video effects: green screen removal, object tracking, text-to-video generation. It's used by studios like Vox and Netflix.

Pricing:

  • Free: 5 projects, 720p exports, watermark
  • Standard: $12/month – 720p exports, 50GB storage
  • Pro: $28/month – 4K exports, 100GB storage, team collaboration
  • Enterprise: Custom

Pros:

  • Cutting-edge AI effects (inpainting, frame interpolation)
  • Excellent for visual effects and compositing

Cons:

  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • Export limits on lower plans can be frustrating

3. Synthesia – AI Avatar Videos Made Easy

Synthesia lets you create videos with AI avatars—just type text, and a realistic presenter speaks it. Perfect for corporate training, sales, and marketing.

Pricing:

  • Starter: $29/month – 1 avatar, 10 minutes of video
  • Pro: $89/month – 3 avatars, 30 minutes, custom templates
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (starting around $500/month)

Pros:

  • No need to film or hire actors
  • Avatars look increasingly realistic

Cons:

  • No free tier—expensive for small creators
  • Limited to avatar-based videos, not traditional editing

4. Pictory – Repurpose Long Content into Short Clips

Pictory is designed for marketers who want to turn blog posts, webinars, or podcasts into short social media videos.

Pricing:

  • Free Trial: 3 videos, watermark
  • Standard: $19/month – 30 videos, text-to-video
  • Premium: $39/month – 60 videos, brand kits, team access

Pros:

  • Great for repurposing content quickly
  • AI automatically extracts highlights

Cons:

  • Fine-tuning is limited
  • Voiceover options are basic

5. InVideo – Best for Social Media Beginners

InVideo offers thousands of templates and an easy drag-and-drop interface. Its AI features include text-to-video and smart trimming.

Pricing:

  • Free: 5 minutes of video per week, watermark
  • Plus: $20/month – 20 minutes/week, no watermark
  • Max: $60/month – Unlimited, priority support

Pros:

  • Huge template library
  • Very beginner-friendly

Cons:

  • Free tier is highly limited
  • Advanced editing features are lacking
