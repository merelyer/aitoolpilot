Detailed Review: DALL-E 3

DALL-E 3, developed by OpenAI, is the latest iteration of their text-to-image model. It's tightly integrated with ChatGPT, meaning you can generate images directly inside a chat interface—no separate app needed. In 2025, DALL-E 3 has improved dramatically in handling complex prompts, especially those involving text rendering (like signs or banners). It also supports inpainting, outpainting, and style variations.

Key Features

  • Text Rendering: Best-in-class for generating readable text in images.
  • ChatGPT Integration: Generate images without leaving your conversation.
  • Inpainting/Outpainting: Edit specific areas or expand the canvas.
  • Style Variations: Apply different artistic styles to existing images.
  • Safety Filters: Robust content moderation (can be restrictive).

Pricing

DALL-E 3 is available through ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) or via OpenAI's API (pay-per-image). The API costs $0.040 per image (standard) and $0.080 per image (HD). There is no free tier, but ChatGPT Plus includes a generous quota of images per month (around 150-200 depending on usage).

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Excellent text accuracy, seamless ChatGPT integration, easy to use, fast generation.
  • Cons: No free tier, limited artistic control, sometimes produces generic results, strict content policies.

Detailed Review: Midjourney

Midjourney remains the darling of the AI art community. It operates exclusively through Discord (though a web interface is in beta in 2025). The platform is known for its dreamy, highly stylized outputs that often rival professional illustrations. Midjourney’s latest model (v6.2 as of early 2025) introduced “character consistency” and “style reference” features, making it easier to maintain a coherent look across a series of images.

Key Features

  • Style Consistency: Use style references to keep a uniform aesthetic.
  • Character Consistency: Generate the same character in multiple poses/scenes.
  • Upscaling: High-resolution outputs up to 4K.
  • Community Gallery: Explore millions of user-generated images for inspiration.
  • Remix Mode: Iterate on existing images with new prompts.

Pricing

Midjourney offers four tiers: Basic ($10/month for 200 images), Standard ($30/month for unlimited images), Pro ($60/month for faster generation and stealth mode), and Mega ($120/month for maximum speed). There is no free tier, but a limited trial is available (25 images).

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Stunning artistic quality, high customization, active community, consistent characters.
  • Cons: Requires Discord, steeper learning curve, slower generation, no built-in text rendering (often fails).
