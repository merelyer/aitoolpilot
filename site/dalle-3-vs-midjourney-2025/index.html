Detailed Review of Each Option

DALL-E 3: The Speed Demon with Text Smarts

DALL-E 3, developed by OpenAI, is the latest iteration of their text-to-image model. It's integrated directly into ChatGPT Plus and also available via the OpenAI API. What sets it apart is its ability to accurately render text within images—a notorious pain point for AI art tools. It's also incredibly fast, generating four images in about 10-15 seconds.

Key Features

  • Text-in-image: Generates readable, contextually placed text (e.g., signs, posters, logos).
  • Natural language understanding: Handles complex, nuanced prompts with ease.
  • Integration: Works inside ChatGPT for iterative editing and conversation-style refinement.
  • Safety filters: Strong content moderation to avoid harmful or biased outputs.

Pricing

  • Free tier: Limited via ChatGPT (2 images per day with free account)
  • ChatGPT Plus: $20/month – unlimited DALL-E 3 generations
  • API: Pay-as-you-go, about $0.04 per image (1024x1024)

Pros

  • Excellent at rendering text – a game-changer for marketing materials
  • Very fast generation times
  • Easy to use, no learning curve
  • Strong safety and ethical guardrails

Cons

  • Artistic quality can feel generic or “plastic” compared to Midjourney
  • Less control over style and composition (no advanced parameters)
  • Limited community features (no gallery or remixing)

Midjourney: The Artist's Powerhouse

Midjourney, now in version 6 (as of early 2025), is the darling of the AI art community. It's accessed via Discord (or a dedicated web interface in beta) and is known for its stunning, painterly, and highly stylized outputs. It gives users granular control through parameters like aspect ratio, stylization, chaos, and more.

Key Features

  • Style versatility: From photorealistic to anime, oil painting to cyberpunk—Midjourney nails it.
  • Advanced parameters: Control chaos, stylize, weird, tile, and more for precise outputs.
  • Remix and variation: Easily iterate on images with subtle or drastic changes.
  • Community gallery: Browse millions of images, see prompts, and remix them.
  • Upscaling: High-resolution upscaling (up to 4x) for print-ready assets.

Pricing

  • Free tier: None (trial ended in 2023)
  • Basic: $10/month – 3.3 hours of GPU time (~200 images)
  • Standard: $30/month – 15 hours GPU time (~900 images)
  • Pro: $60/month – 30 hours GPU time (~1800 images)
  • Enterprise: $120/month – 60 hours GPU time, priority support

Pros

  • Superior artistic quality and stylistic range
  • Granular control over output parameters
  • Active community with endless inspiration
  • Excellent for character design, concept art, and unique visuals

Cons

  • Steeper learning curve (Discord interface, parameter syntax)
  • Slower generation (15-30 seconds per image set)
  • Text rendering is hit-or-miss (often garbled)
  • No direct API for developers (yet)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DALL-E 3 better than Midjourney?

It depends on your needs. DALL-E 3 is better for text rendering, speed, and ease of use. Midjourney is better for artistic quality, style variety, and control.

Can I use DALL-E 3 for free?

Yes, but limited. With a free ChatGPT account, you can generate up to 2 images per day using DALL-E 3. For unlimited use, you need ChatGPT Plus ($20/month).

Does Midjourney have a free trial?

No, Midjourney ended its free trial in 2023. You must subscribe to a paid plan starting at $10/month.

Which AI art tool is best for text in images?

DALL-E 3 is the clear winner for generating readable, accurate text inside images. Midjourney often produces garbled or nonsensical text.

Can I use Midjourney for commercial projects?

Yes, Midjourney's terms allow commercial use of generated images for all paid subscribers. However, if you earn over $1M/year, you need a Pro or Enterprise plan.

Which tool is faster: DALL-E 3 or Midjourney?

DALL-E 3 is significantly faster, generating four images in about 10-15 seconds. Midjourney takes 15-30 seconds for a set of four images.

Can I generate images in different styles with DALL-E 3?

Yes, DALL-E 3 can produce various styles (photorealistic, cartoon, oil painting, etc.), but the range and quality are narrower compared to Midjourney.

Is Midjourney worth the price in 2025?

Absolutely, if you value high-quality, unique art and don't mind the learning curve. The Basic plan at $10/month is a steal for hobbyists, while professionals may need Standard or Pro.
