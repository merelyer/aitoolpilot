Detailed Review: Notion AI

Overview

Notion AI is an add-on to the popular all-in-one workspace. It is designed to help you write, summarize, brainstorm, and edit content directly inside your pages. It's particularly strong for teams that live in documents—like product teams, writers, and educators.

Key Features

  • AI-powered writing assistant (draft, continue writing, fix spelling, change tone)
  • Summarize long pages or meeting notes
  • Generate action items from text
  • Ask AI questions about your workspace (Q&A)
  • Translate content into multiple languages

Pricing

Notion's base plans: Free (limited), Plus ($10/member/mo), Business ($18/member/mo), Enterprise (custom). The AI add-on costs $10 per member per month (annual) or $12 monthly. Yes, it's pricey—especially for large teams.

Pros

  • Excellent writing and summarization quality
  • Seamless integration with Notion's database and pages
  • Great for knowledge management and company wikis
  • Flexible block-based editor

Cons

  • Expensive per-user AI add-on
  • Limited project management features (no built-in Gantt, time tracking)
  • AI can hallucinate or give vague answers
  • No native AI-powered automation for tasks

Detailed Review: ClickUp AI

Overview

ClickUp AI is baked into the ClickUp platform, which is a robust project management tool. It helps you write tasks, generate project briefs, automate workflows, and get insights. It's ideal for teams that need to manage complex projects and want AI to speed up repetitive work.

Key Features

  • AI writing for tasks, docs, and comments
  • Automated project updates and status summaries
  • Generate action items from meetings
  • Create subtasks and assignees from text
  • AI-powered search across the workspace
  • Integration with over 1,000 apps

Pricing

ClickUp base plans: Free Forever (limited), Unlimited ($10/member/mo), Business ($19/member/mo), Enterprise (custom). The AI add-on is $5 per member per month (annual) or $7 monthly—half the price of Notion AI.

Pros

  • More affordable AI add-on
  • Deep integration with tasks, sprints, and goals
  • Strong automation capabilities (AI + no-code)
  • Built-in time tracking, Gantt, and whiteboards
  • Better for team collaboration on projects

Cons

  • Steeper learning curve due to many features
  • AI writing quality sometimes less polished than Notion
  • Interface can feel cluttered
  • Less effective for pure document-heavy use

Who Should Use What

Choose Notion AI if:

  • Your team lives in documents—wikis, meeting notes, product specs
  • You need high-quality writing and summarization
  • You prefer a clean, minimalist interface
  • You're a writer, educator, or content team

Choose ClickUp AI if:

  • You need robust project management (tasks, sprints, Gantt)
  • You want to automate workflows and generate tasks from text
  • Your team is larger and budget-conscious
  • You value integrations and custom views

Bottom Line Verdict

Both tools are excellent, but they serve different primary use cases. ClickUp AI offers better value for money and deeper project management capabilities. Notion AI is superior for knowledge-centric teams. If you're torn, consider a trial of both—or use both together (Notion for docs, ClickUp for tasks) if your budget allows. For most teams, ClickUp AI is the productivity booster you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Notion AI and ClickUp AI be used together?

Yes, you can use both simultaneously. Use Notion for your knowledge base and documentation, and ClickUp for project management and task automation. However, this will increase costs as you pay for both subscriptions.

Which AI tool is better for writing long-form content?

Notion AI is generally better for long-form writing. Its AI is more tuned to drafting articles, reports, and documentation. ClickUp AI excels at shorter, task-oriented writing.

Is ClickUp AI worth the extra cost over Notion AI?

ClickUp AI is actually cheaper ($5/member/mo vs $10 for Notion AI). If you already use ClickUp for project management, the AI add-on is a no-brainer. For Notion users, the AI add-on is more expensive but offers superior writing quality.

Can I try the AI features for free?

Both tools offer free trials. Notion AI gives a limited number of AI responses on the free plan. ClickUp AI offers a 7-day free trial on paid plans. Check their websites for current offers.

Which tool is easier to learn?

Notion AI has a gentler learning curve due to its simpler interface. ClickUp AI offers more features but can be overwhelming initially. Both have extensive documentation and community support.

Do these AI tools support multiple languages?

Yes, both support multiple languages. Notion AI can translate and generate text in several major languages. ClickUp AI also handles multiple languages, though its strength is primarily in English.
