Detailed Review of Each Option

Notion AI: The Intelligent Workspace

Notion has evolved from a simple note-taking app into a full-fledged workspace. The AI add-on (released in 2023 and refined through 2025) brings generative AI directly into your pages. You can ask it to draft a blog post, summarize meeting notes, or even brainstorm project ideas. The AI is context-aware—it pulls from your existing database to generate relevant content.

Key Features:

  • AI Writing: Generate, edit, and translate text within any page.
  • AI Q&A: Ask questions about your workspace and get answers from your notes.
  • Autofill: Fill database properties automatically based on context.
  • Summarize: Condense long documents into bullet points.

Pricing: Notion’s free plan is generous for individuals, but the AI add-on costs $10/month per member (billed annually). You also need at least the Plus plan ($10/month per member) to use AI. So, for a team of 5, that’s $100/month total.

Pros:

  • Exceptional for knowledge bases and wikis.
  • AI is deeply integrated into the writing experience.
  • Clean, distraction-free interface.

Cons:

  • AI is an add-on, not part of the base plan.
  • Limited project management features compared to ClickUp.
  • No native time tracking or Gantt charts (without integrations).

ClickUp AI: The All-in-One Productivity Hub

ClickUp started as a project management tool and has aggressively added AI capabilities. In 2025, ClickUp AI is baked into every part of the platform—tasks, docs, whiteboards, and even sprints. You can generate task descriptions, write project updates, create subtasks from a single prompt, and get smart suggestions for task priorities.

Key Features:

  • AI Writing: Draft emails, docs, and comments directly within ClickUp.
  • AI Task Management: Generate tasks, subtasks, and dependencies from plain English.
  • AI Summarization: Summarize long threads, docs, or meeting notes.
  • AI Automation: Create and suggest automation rules based on your workflow.

Pricing: ClickUp has a robust free plan (100MB storage, unlimited tasks). The AI add-on costs $7/month per user (billed annually) on top of any paid plan. The Unlimited plan (which includes most features) is $10/month per user. So for a team of 5, that’s $85/month total—cheaper than Notion AI.

Pros:

  • AI is embedded in task management, not just docs.
  • Excellent automation and integrations.
  • More affordable for teams.

Cons:

  • Steeper learning curve due to many features.
  • Interface can feel cluttered.
  • AI sometimes generates generic responses.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Notion AI free?

No, Notion AI is a paid add-on costing $10/month per user (billed annually). You also need a paid Notion plan (starting at $10/month per user) to use it.

2. Does ClickUp AI have a free trial?

Yes, ClickUp offers a free plan with limited AI credits. You can also start a free trial of the AI add-on for 7 days on any paid plan.

3. Which tool has better AI writing features?

Notion AI excels at long-form writing and knowledge-based content. ClickUp AI is better for short-form task descriptions, emails, and project updates. Your choice depends on your primary use case.

4. Can I use Notion AI for project management?

You can, but it’s not as robust as ClickUp. Notion lacks native Gantt charts, time tracking, and advanced dependency management. ClickUp is built for project management from the ground up.

5. Which tool is better for a solo freelancer?

For a freelancer, Notion AI might be more intuitive if you focus on content creation and client wikis. However, ClickUp AI’s free plan offers more project management features without extra cost.

6. How does AI handle data privacy?

Both tools use AI models that process your data. Notion states it doesn’t train on your workspace data. ClickUp similarly has data protection measures. Always check their latest privacy policies for details.

7. Can I switch from Notion to ClickUp easily?

Yes, ClickUp offers native import tools for Notion. You can migrate your pages, databases, and tasks with a few clicks. Expect some formatting adjustments.
