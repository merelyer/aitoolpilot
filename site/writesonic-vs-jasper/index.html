Detailed Review of Writesonic

Overview

Writesonic burst onto the scene in 2021 and has since evolved into a full-featured AI writing suite. It’s built on GPT-4 and proprietary models, offering over 100 templates for blog posts, ads, product descriptions, emails, and more. Its standout feature is Chatsonic, a ChatGPT-like assistant that can browse the web for real-time data.

Key Features

  • Chatsonic: Real-time web browsing, image generation (via Stable Diffusion), and voice commands.
  • SEO Mode: Integrates with Surfer SEO to optimize content for keywords.
  • Brand Voice: Customize tone and style per project.
  • AI Article Writer 5.0: Generates 2,000+ word articles with one click.
  • Plagiarism Checker: Built-in Copyscape integration (paid add-on).
  • Zapier Integration: Connect with 5,000+ apps.

Pricing

  • Free Tier: 10,000 words/month, limited features.
  • Starter: $19/month (annual) – 60,000 words, 100+ templates.
  • Professional: $59/month (annual) – unlimited words, 3 brand voices, SEO mode.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing – dedicated support, API access.

Pros

  • Excellent value for money—lowest entry price among top tools.
  • Real-time web browsing in Chatsonic (Jasper can't do this).
  • Strong ecommerce templates (product descriptions, Amazon ads).
  • Generous free trial (10,000 words).

Cons

  • Output quality can vary; sometimes needs heavy editing.
  • Brand voice feature is less sophisticated than Jasper's.
  • Plagiarism checker is a paid extra.

Detailed Review of Jasper

Overview

Jasper (formerly Conversion.ai) is the veteran of AI writing. It’s trusted by big brands like Airbnb and IBM. Jasper is built on GPT-4 and focuses on long-form content with deep brand voice customization. It also includes Jasper Art for AI image generation and Jasper Chat for conversational AI.

Key Features

  • Brand Voice: Train Jasper on your existing content to match your exact tone.
  • Jasper Art: Text-to-image generator (DALL-E 2 based).
  • Jasper Chat: Context-aware assistant that remembers previous conversations.
  • Recipes: Pre-built workflows for specific tasks (e.g., blog post, press release).
  • SEO Mode: Integrates with Surfer SEO (paid add-on).
  • Browser Extension: Write across the web (Gmail, Google Docs, etc.).
  • API Access: Enterprise only.

Pricing

  • Free Tier: 7-day trial (no credit card required) with limited words.
  • Creator: $49/month (annual) – 1 brand voice, 50+ templates, 1 user.
  • Pro: $99/month (annual) – 3 brand voices, 3 users, Jasper Art included.
  • Business: Custom pricing – unlimited users, API, dedicated account manager.

Pros

  • Superior brand voice customization—best for consistent tone.
  • Excellent long-form content generation (2,000+ words).
  • Jasper Art is a nice all-in-one addition.
  • Strong enterprise features and support.

Cons

  • Expensive—starting price is more than double Writesonic.
  • No real-time web browsing (Jasper Chat is offline).
  • Free trial is short (7 days) and limited.
  • Steep learning curve for advanced features.

Who Should Use What

Choose Writesonic if:

  • You’re a solopreneur or small business on a tight budget.
  • You need ecommerce content (product descriptions, Amazon listings).
  • You want real-time web browsing for fact-checking or news articles.
  • You prefer a generous free trial to test the waters.

Choose Jasper if:

  • You’re a marketing team or enterprise with a larger budget.
  • Brand voice consistency is your top priority.
  • You produce long-form content (eBooks, white papers) regularly.
  • You need an all-in-one solution with image generation.

Bottom Line Verdict

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Writesonic better than Jasper?

For most users, yes. Writesonic is more affordable, offers a free tier, and includes real-time web browsing. However, Jasper is better for deep brand voice consistency and long-form content.

Can I use Writesonic for free?

Yes, Writesonic offers a free tier with 10,000 words per month, which is more generous than Jasper's 7-day trial.

Which tool is better for SEO content?

Both have SEO modes with Surfer SEO integration, but Writesonic’s SEO mode is included in the Professional plan, while Jasper’s is a paid add-on. Writesonic wins on value.

Does Jasper have a free trial?

Yes, Jasper offers a 7-day free trial with no credit card required, but the word limit is relatively low compared to Writesonic's free tier.

Can I use these tools for ecommerce product descriptions?

Yes, both tools have templates for product descriptions. Writesonic has more specialized ecommerce templates, making it the better choice for online stores.

Which tool has better customer support?

Both offer email and chat support. Jasper’s enterprise support is more comprehensive, but Writesonic’s support is faster for standard plans.
