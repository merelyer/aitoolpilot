In this detailed comparison, we break down Synthesia vs HeyGen pricing and features in 2025, so you can decide which tool fits your budget and workflow.

TL;DR: Our #1 Pick
For most businesses, Synthesia is the better all-around choice due to its superior avatar quality, robust template library, and transparent pricing. Detailed Review of Synthesia

Synthesia has been a leader in AI video generation since 2017. It offers over 140 AI avatars, supports 120+ languages, and is trusted by companies like Amazon, Nike, and Reuters. The platform is built for scale—perfect for creating training modules, product demos, and internal communications.

Key Features

  • Realistic AI Avatars: Over 140 diverse avatars, including custom avatar creation (enterprise only).
  • Multi-Language Support: 120+ languages and accents with natural-sounding voices.
  • Video Templates: 60+ professionally designed templates for common use cases.
  • Screen Recording: Record your screen and add an AI avatar presenter.
  • Collaboration: Team workspaces, shared assets, and review/approval workflows.
  • Integrations: API access, Zapier, and LMS (Learning Management System) connectors.

Detailed Review of HeyGen

HeyGen (formerly known as Movio) burst onto the scene in 2022 and quickly gained popularity for its user-friendly interface and budget-friendly plans. It offers over 100 avatars, supports 40+ languages, and is ideal for social media content, explainer videos, and quick marketing clips.

Key Features

  • AI Avatars: 100+ avatars, including photo and video avatars (custom avatar available on Pro+).
  • Voice Cloning: Clone your own voice for personalized videos (Pro plan).
  • Text to Video: Simple script editor with instant preview.
  • Video Templates: 50+ templates for social media, ads, and education.
  • AI Script Generator: Generate scripts from prompts (limited).
  • API Access: Available on Team plan and above.

Who Should Use What

Synthesia is best for:

  • Large corporations needing high-quality, multilingual training videos.
  • Marketing teams producing polished product demos and explainers.
  • Organizations requiring custom avatars and enterprise-grade security.
  • Users who prioritize avatar realism and lip-sync accuracy above all.

HeyGen is best for:

  • Solo entrepreneurs and content creators on a budget.
  • Small businesses needing quick, simple videos for social media.
  • Users who want to test AI video generation without a big investment.
  • Anyone needing voice cloning for personalized video messages.

Bottom Line Verdict

Both Synthesia and HeyGen are excellent AI video tools, but they cater to different audiences. Synthesia is the premium option—more expensive but with superior avatar quality, broader language support, and enterprise features. HeyGen is the value champion, offering a lower entry price, voice cloning, and unlimited videos on the Team plan. If your budget allows, Synthesia is the safer long-term investment for professional video production. If you're just starting out or need to produce high volumes of simple videos, HeyGen is a fantastic choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is cheaper, Synthesia or HeyGen?

HeyGen is cheaper at the entry level—its Creator plan costs $24/month (or $19/month billed annually), while Synthesia's Starter plan is $29/month ($22.50 annually). However, for unlimited videos, HeyGen's Team plan ($49/month) is more affordable than Synthesia's Pro plan ($89/month).

Does Synthesia or HeyGen have better avatar quality?

Synthesia generally offers more realistic avatars with better lip-sync accuracy and natural movement. HeyGen's avatars are good but can sometimes appear slightly robotic. For professional corporate videos, Synthesia is the winner.

Can I use Synthesia or HeyGen for free?

Both tools offer a free tier that lets you create one video (up to 1 minute) with a watermark. For unlimited use, you need a paid plan.

Which tool supports more languages?

Synthesia supports 120+ languages, while HeyGen supports around 40. If you need to create videos in many languages, Synthesia is the clear choice.

Can I create custom avatars with Synthesia or HeyGen?

Both offer custom avatars, but only on higher-tier plans. Synthesia requires an Enterprise plan (custom pricing), while HeyGen offers custom avatars on its Team plan ($49/month) and above.

Which is better for social media videos?

HeyGen is better for quick social media clips due to its lower cost and faster rendering. Synthesia is better for polished, long-form content like tutorials and product demos.
