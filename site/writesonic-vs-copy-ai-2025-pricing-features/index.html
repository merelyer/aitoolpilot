Detailed Review: Writesonic

Writesonic has evolved from a simple copywriting tool into a full-featured content generation platform. It now includes a built-in blog writer, SEO checker (powered by Surfer SEO), AI image generation, and even a chatbot-like interface called ChatSonic.

Key Features

  • Long-form editor: Write 1,500+ word articles with outlines, headings, and bullet points.
  • SEO integration: Real-time keyword suggestions, readability scores, and content optimization (requires premium plan).
  • 25+ templates: From product descriptions to Google Ads, email subject lines to press releases.
  • Brand voice: Customize tone and style to match your brand.
  • AI image generator: Create visuals directly inside the editor.
  • ChatSonic: A conversational AI assistant for brainstorming, rewriting, or answering questions.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: 10,000 words/month, limited templates, no SEO.
  • Starter: $19/month (annual) — 30,000 words, 1 brand voice, 10+ templates.
  • Professional: $49/month (annual) — 100,000 words, SEO mode, 3 brand voices, priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing — unlimited words, advanced security, dedicated account manager.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Excellent long-form output, built-in SEO, versatile templates, good value for money.
  • Cons: Steep learning curve for advanced features, sometimes generates repetitive content, free plan is limiting.

Detailed Review: Copy.ai

Copy.ai focuses on speed and simplicity. It’s designed for users who want to generate high-quality short-form copy in seconds. With a clean interface and a growing set of features, it’s a favorite among social media managers and sales teams.

Key Features

  • 90+ templates: Covers social media posts, email sequences, landing page copy, blog intros, and more.
  • Infobase: Store brand details, product info, and style guides for consistent output.
  • AI Chat: A conversational mode for brainstorming and refining ideas.
  • Browser extension: Use Copy.ai anywhere on the web.
  • Workflows: Automate multi-step content creation (e.g., generate a social post, then an email, then a landing page).
  • Language support: 25+ languages.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: 2,000 words/month, limited templates, watermark on exports.
  • Starter (formerly Pro): $49/month (annual) — unlimited words, all templates, 5 seats, Infobase, workflows.
  • Advanced: $249/month (annual) — unlimited seats, custom workflows, API access, priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing — SSO, dedicated support, custom contracts.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Extremely easy to use, fast output, great for short-form copy, solid free trial.
  • Cons: Weak long-form writing, no built-in SEO, higher starting price than Writesonic, limited customization.

Who Should Use What

Choose Writesonic if:

  • You write blog posts or long-form articles regularly.
  • SEO is a priority — you want keyword suggestions and readability analysis.
  • You need an all-in-one tool (writing + images + chat).
  • You’re on a tight budget and want maximum value.

Choose Copy.ai if:

  • Your main focus is social media, ads, or email copy.
  • You value speed and simplicity over advanced features.
  • You want a clean, intuitive interface with minimal learning curve.
  • You need a tool that integrates easily with your existing workflows.

Bottom Line Verdict

Both Writesonic and Copy.ai are excellent AI writing tools, but they serve different audiences. For content marketers, bloggers, and SEO-focused teams, Writesonic is the clear winner — it offers better long-form capabilities, SEO tools, and a lower price point. For social media managers, sales teams, and anyone who needs quick, high-quality short-form copy, Copy.ai is a fantastic choice thanks to its simplicity and speed.

If you’re still on the fence, try both free plans. Start with Writesonic if you write long content, or Copy.ai if you need fast short-form copy. Either way, you’ll save time and improve your content quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main difference between Writesonic and Copy.ai?

Writesonic is better for long-form content like blog posts and includes built-in SEO tools, while Copy.ai excels at short-form copy like social media posts and ads with a simpler interface.

Which tool is more affordable?

Writesonic is more affordable, with a starter plan at $19/month (annual) compared to Copy.ai’s $49/month (annual). Writesonic also offers a more generous free tier (10,000 words vs 2,000 words).

Can I use Writesonic or Copy.ai for SEO writing?

Writesonic has a dedicated SEO mode with keyword suggestions and readability analysis (requires Professional plan). Copy.ai does not offer built-in SEO features.

Do these tools support multiple languages?

Yes, both support 25+ languages. Copy.ai has a slight edge in language accuracy for some European languages, but Writesonic is also very solid.

Which tool is better for beginners?

Copy.ai is easier for beginners due to its clean interface and simple workflows. Writesonic has more features, which can be overwhelming at first, but it offers more flexibility once learned.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Both tools offer month-to-month billing and allow cancellation anytime. Annual plans are cheaper but may have limited refunds.

Do these tools have a free trial?

Yes, both offer free plans. Writesonic gives 10,000 words/month forever, while Copy.ai offers 2,000 words/month. No credit card required for either.
