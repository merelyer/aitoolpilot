Detailed Review of Each Option

Midjourney

Midjourney is an AI image generator that has become synonymous with high-quality, artistic visuals. It operates through Discord, which can be a barrier for some, but the results speak for themselves. In 2025, Midjourney has refined its algorithms to produce images that are often indistinguishable from professional photography or digital art.

Key Features

  • Version 6 & beyond: Midjourney's latest models offer incredible detail, lighting, and texture.
  • Style customization: Use parameters like --ar for aspect ratio, --stylize for artistic intensity, and --v for model version.
  • Image-to-image: Upload an image as a reference for the AI to base its generation on.
  • Inpainting & outpainting: Edit specific parts of an image or expand the canvas (available in advanced plans).
  • Community gallery: Browse millions of generated images for inspiration.

Pricing

  • Basic Plan: $10/month — 3.3 hours of GPU time, commercial use, 10 concurrent jobs.
  • Standard Plan: $30/month — 15 hours of GPU time, 15 concurrent jobs, relax mode.
  • Pro Plan: $60/month — 30 hours of GPU time, unlimited relax mode, 3 concurrent fast jobs.
  • Mega Plan: $120/month — 60 hours of GPU time, priority support.

Pros

  • Unmatched artistic quality and stylistic variety.
  • Active community and constant updates.
  • Excellent for generating concept art, characters, and environments.
  • Commercial use included in all paid plans.

Cons

  • Requires Discord — no standalone web app.
  • Steeper learning curve with text-based commands.
  • Less accurate with complex, multi-object prompts compared to DALL-E 3.
  • No free tier (only a trial of 25 images).

DALL-E 3

DALL-E 3, developed by OpenAI, is the latest iteration of the groundbreaking image generator. It is deeply integrated with ChatGPT, making it incredibly user-friendly. DALL-E 3 focuses on understanding and executing complex prompts with high precision and photorealism.

Key Features

  • ChatGPT integration: Use natural language to generate and refine images within ChatGPT.
  • Advanced prompt adherence: DALL-E 3 is far better at following detailed, multi-part instructions.
  • Photorealism: Excels at generating realistic images of people, objects, and scenes.
  • Inpainting: Select and edit parts of an image with simple text descriptions.
  • Safety filters: Built-in content moderation to prevent misuse.

Pricing

  • Free Tier: Limited generations via ChatGPT (slower, lower resolution).
  • ChatGPT Plus: $20/month — Access to DALL-E 3, faster generation, priority access.
  • ChatGPT Pro: $200/month — Unlimited DALL-E 3 generations, highest quality.
  • API Pricing: $0.040 per image (standard), $0.080 per image (HD).

Pros

  • Exceptional prompt understanding — great for complex instructions.
  • User-friendly interface via ChatGPT.
  • High photorealism and accurate representation of text in images.
  • Free tier available for casual use.

Cons

  • Less artistic variety — images can feel generic or overly perfect.
  • Limited style control compared to Midjourney's parameters.
  • Requires ChatGPT subscription for best results.
  • Slower generation speed than Midjourney in practice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for realistic images, Midjourney or DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 generally produces more photorealistic images with better accuracy in lighting, shadows, and anatomical details. Midjourney can also create realistic images, but it often adds an artistic flair that may not suit all projects.

Can I use Midjourney for free?

Midjourney offers a limited trial of 25 images for new users. After that, you need a paid subscription starting at $10/month. There is no permanent free tier.

Is DALL-E 3 included with ChatGPT Plus?

Yes, DALL-E 3 is available to ChatGPT Plus subscribers ($20/month). You can access it directly in the chat interface to generate and refine images.

Which tool is better for commercial use?

Both tools allow commercial use. Midjourney includes commercial rights in all paid plans. DALL-E 3 also permits commercial use, but you must own the content generated via ChatGPT or the API.

Can I edit images after generation in Midjourney and DALL-E 3?

Yes, both offer inpainting/editing features. Midjourney's editing is more advanced with its Vary (Region) tool, while DALL-E 3 allows you to select and modify parts of an image via ChatGPT.

Which AI image generator is faster?

Midjourney is generally faster for generating images, especially in Fast mode, taking about 30-60 seconds per image. DALL-E 3 can take 1-2 minutes per generation, depending on server load.
