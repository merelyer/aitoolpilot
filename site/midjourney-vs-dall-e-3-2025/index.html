Detailed Review of Midjourney

Features & Capabilities

Midjourney operates exclusively through Discord, offering a unique social experience. Key features in 2025 include:

  • Version 6.1 with improved coherence, texture, and lighting
  • Style customization via parameters like --style raw, --stylize, --weird
  • Upscaling and remix for refining outputs
  • Image-to-image and inpainting (beta)
  • Community galleries for inspiration

Pricing (as of 2025)

Midjourney offers four tiers, all billed monthly:

  • Basic: $10/month – 3.3 hours of GPU time, limited concurrent jobs
  • Standard: $30/month – 15 hours GPU time, relaxed mode
  • Pro: $60/month – 30 hours GPU time, stealth mode, faster queue
  • Mega: $120/month – 60 hours GPU time, priority support

Pros

  • Stunning artistic quality with unique styles
  • Strong community and constant updates
  • Excellent for concept art and fantasy themes

Cons

  • Requires Discord (learning curve)
  • No free tier (except limited trials)
  • Less photorealistic than DALL-E 3
  • Occasional bias in character generation

Detailed Review of DALL-E 3

Features & Capabilities

DALL-E 3 is integrated into ChatGPT (Plus/Pro) and also available via OpenAI API. Key features:

  • ChatGPT integration – generate images from natural language conversations
  • Photorealism with accurate lighting, shadows, and textures
  • Text rendering – much better than Midjourney for logos and signs
  • Safety filters – stricter content moderation
  • Image editing (inpainting, outpainting) within ChatGPT

Pricing (as of 2025)

  • Free tier: Limited images via ChatGPT (slow, watermarked)
  • ChatGPT Plus: $20/month – faster generation, priority access
  • ChatGPT Pro: $200/month – unlimited, highest priority
  • API: Pay-per-image (approx. $0.04 per image)

Pros

  • Easiest to use with ChatGPT
  • Best for photorealistic and commercial-safe images
  • Free tier available

Cons

  • Less artistic freedom and style variety
  • Strict content filters (can be limiting)
  • No dedicated community platform
  • Higher cost for heavy users (API)

FAQ

Which is better, Midjourney or DALL-E 3?

It depends on your needs. Midjourney excels at artistic, stylized images with a strong community. DALL-E 3 is better for photorealistic, safe-for-work images and integrates seamlessly with ChatGPT. For most commercial use, DALL-E 3 wins; for creative projects, Midjourney is superior.

Is DALL-E 3 free in 2025?

Yes, DALL-E 3 has a limited free tier through ChatGPT. You can generate a few images per day, but they may be watermarked and slower. For unlimited access, you need ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) or Pro ($200/month).

Can Midjourney generate photorealistic images?

Yes, but it's not its strongest suit. Midjourney can produce realistic images with the right prompts and parameters (like --style raw), but DALL-E 3 generally delivers more accurate photorealism, especially for human faces and everyday objects.

Which AI image generator is best for commercial use?

DALL-E 3 is better for commercial use due to its stricter content safety and photorealistic output. However, check the terms of service for each platform; both allow commercial use with paid plans, but Midjourney's terms are more restrictive for certain uses (e.g., generating NSFW content is prohibited).

What are the main differences in output quality?

Midjourney produces more artistic, painterly, and sometimes surreal images with a distinct style. DALL-E 3 focuses on realism, accurate lighting, and text rendering. For logos, signs, and stock photos, DALL-E 3 is better. For fantasy art, concept design, and abstract visuals, Midjourney is the winner.

Can I use Midjourney without Discord?

Currently, Midjourney is only accessible via Discord. There is no standalone web interface, though the company has hinted at a web app in development. For now, you must use Discord to generate images.
