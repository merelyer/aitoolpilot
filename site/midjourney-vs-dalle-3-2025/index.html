Detailed Review of Midjourney

Midjourney, now in its sixth iteration as of 2025, remains the gold standard for AI-generated art. It operates as a Discord bot, which some find clunky, but the community-driven approach is a feature, not a bug.

Key Features

  • Version 6 (v6) Model: Offers unprecedented detail, better understanding of complex prompts, and improved lighting and texture.
  • Stylized Outputs: Midjourney excels at creating images with a distinct artistic style — think concept art, fantasy landscapes, and character designs.
  • Upscaling & Remixing: Built-in upscaling up to 4x resolution, plus a remix feature that allows you to tweak details without starting from scratch.
  • Community Gallery: Access to millions of public images and prompts for inspiration.

Pricing

Midjourney offers three main subscription tiers:

  • Basic Plan: $10/month — 3.3 hours of GPU time, commercial use license, and access to community gallery.
  • Standard Plan: $30/month — 15 hours of GPU time, faster generation, and the ability to relax mode (unlimited slower generations).
  • Pro Plan: $60/month — 30 hours of GPU time, priority support, and stealth mode (private image generation).

Pros

  • Unmatched artistic quality and detail
  • Strong community and prompt-sharing culture
  • Advanced features like remixing and upscaling
  • Constant updates and improvements

Cons

  • Requires Discord — not a standalone web app
  • Steeper learning curve for prompt engineering
  • Limited to 4:3 or square aspect ratios (though custom aspect ratios are possible)
  • No free tier beyond a limited trial

Detailed Review of DALL-E 3

DALL-E 3, developed by OpenAI, is the most user-friendly AI image generator on the market. Integrated into ChatGPT Plus and available via API, it's designed for speed and accuracy.

Key Features

  • Native Integration with ChatGPT: You can generate images directly in the ChatGPT interface, making it ideal for conversational workflows.
  • Photorealism: DALL-E 3 produces stunningly realistic images, especially for portraits, products, and scenes.
  • Text Rendering: Significantly improved ability to render text within images — a game-changer for marketers.
  • Safety & Moderation: Built-in content filters reduce the risk of generating inappropriate or harmful images.

Pricing

  • Free Tier: Limited generation via Bing Image Creator (slower, lower resolution).
  • ChatGPT Plus: $20/month — includes DALL-E 3 access with faster generation and higher resolution.
  • API: $0.04 per image (standard resolution), $0.08 per image (HD). Volume discounts available.

Pros

  • Extremely easy to use — no Discord needed
  • Excellent for photorealistic and commercial images
  • Good text rendering capabilities
  • Integrated with ChatGPT for seamless workflows

Cons

  • Less artistic control compared to Midjourney
  • Lower maximum resolution (1024x1024 standard, up to 1792x1024 with HD)
  • Limited style variety — outputs tend to look similar

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Midjourney better than DALL-E 3?

It depends on your goals. Midjourney offers superior artistic quality and control, while DALL-E 3 is easier to use and better for photorealistic images. For most creative professionals, Midjourney is the better choice.

2. Can I use DALL-E 3 for free?

Yes, you can use DALL-E 3 for free via Bing Image Creator, but with limitations on resolution and generation speed. For full access, you need a ChatGPT Plus subscription ($20/month).

3. Which AI image generator is best for commercial use?

Both tools allow commercial use with their paid plans. Midjourney's terms are more permissive for selling art, while DALL-E 3's terms are clear for marketing materials. Always check the latest licensing terms.

4. How do I write better prompts for Midjourney vs DALL-E 3?

For Midjourney, use descriptive artistic terms (e.g., "cinematic lighting, octane render, trending on ArtStation"). For DALL-E 3, use natural language and be specific about the subject (e.g., "a realistic photo of a golden retriever sitting on a red couch").

5. Which AI image generator has better resolution?

Midjourney offers higher maximum resolution (up to 4x upscaling), while DALL-E 3 maxes out at 1792x1024. For print or large displays, Midjourney is superior.

6. Can I generate images with text using Midjourney?

Midjourney's text rendering has improved but is still not as reliable as DALL-E 3. If you need accurate text in images, DALL-E 3 is the better choice.

7. Which tool is more affordable for heavy users?

For heavy users, Midjourney's Standard Plan ($30/month) offers unlimited relax mode, which can be more cost-effective than DALL-E 3's API pricing if you generate hundreds of images per month.
