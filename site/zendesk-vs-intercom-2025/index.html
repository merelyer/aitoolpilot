Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which is cheaper, Zendesk or Intercom?

Zendesk is significantly cheaper, starting at $55/agent/month compared to Intercom's $74/agent/month. For larger teams, the price gap widens dramatically.

2. Which platform has better AI for customer support?

Both are strong. Zendesk's AI excels at ticket triage and deflection, while Intercom's Fin AI is better for conversational, real-time support. Your choice depends on your support model.

3. Can I use Zendesk for in-app messaging like Intercom?

Yes, Zendesk offers a chat widget and in-app messaging, but it's not as seamless or feature-rich as Intercom's. Intercom is built for in-app experiences.

4. Do both platforms offer a free trial?

Yes, both offer 14-day free trials. No credit card is required for Zendesk; Intercom may require one for some plans.

5. Which is better for a small business with a limited budget?

Zendesk is better for small businesses due to lower starting prices and more inclusive AI features. Intercom's value proposition is stronger for businesses with a higher customer lifetime value.

6. Can I migrate from Intercom to Zendesk (or vice versa)?

Yes, both platforms offer migration tools and support. However, data migration can be complex, especially for custom workflows and chatbots. Plan for a few weeks of transition.

7. Which platform has better reporting and analytics?

Intercom offers more intuitive, user-friendly analytics for customer behavior. Zendesk's reporting is more powerful but has a steeper learning curve.
