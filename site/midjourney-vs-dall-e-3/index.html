Detailed Review: Midjourney

Midjourney has been the darling of the AI art community since its beta. It operates entirely through Discord, which can be a hurdle, but the results speak for themselves.

Key Features

  • Style & Aesthetics: Midjourney excels at generating painterly, cinematic, and highly stylized images. It handles lighting, texture, and composition like a pro.
  • Customization: You can tweak parameters like aspect ratio, style weight, and chaos level. Advanced users can use “remix” mode to iterate.
  • Community & Galleries: A vibrant Discord community shares prompts and techniques. You can browse public galleries for inspiration.
  • Upscaling & Variation: Built-in upscalers (up to 4x) and easy variation generation make it perfect for refining ideas.

Pricing

  • Basic Plan: $10/month – 3.3 hours of GPU time (approx. 200 images)
  • Standard Plan: $30/month – 15 hours GPU time (unlimited relax mode)
  • Pro Plan: $60/month – 30 hours GPU time (stealth mode + priority queue)
  • Mega Plan: $120/month – 60 hours GPU time (for heavy users)

Pros

  • Unmatched artistic quality and style versatility
  • Robust customization and parameter control
  • Active community for learning and sharing
  • Regular updates (v6.1 is excellent)

Cons

  • Requires Discord – steep learning curve for some
  • No free tier (trial is limited)
  • Can be slow during peak hours on lower plans
  • Less accurate with text rendering than DALL-E 3

Detailed Review: DALL-E 3

OpenAI’s DALL-E 3 is integrated into ChatGPT and offers a more user-friendly experience. It’s designed for speed and accuracy, especially when it comes to following complex prompts.

Key Features

  • Prompt Adherence: DALL-E 3 is remarkably good at understanding detailed prompts, including specific objects, colors, and compositions.
  • Text Rendering: It can generate readable text within images (e.g., signs, logos), which Midjourney struggles with.
  • ChatGPT Integration: You can generate images directly in ChatGPT (Plus or Enterprise) without switching tools. It can also refine prompts conversationally.
  • Safety & Content Filtering: Strong guardrails against harmful content, but can be overly restrictive.

Pricing

  • Free Tier: Limited access via Bing Image Creator (slower, lower quality)
  • ChatGPT Plus: $20/month – includes DALL-E 3 access with faster generation
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing – higher limits and admin controls

Pros

  • Easiest to use – no Discord needed, integrated with ChatGPT
  • Excellent prompt following and text generation
  • Affordable entry point (ChatGPT Plus covers many tools)
  • Fast generation (even on free tier via Bing)

Cons

  • Less artistic “soul” – images can feel generic or overly clean
  • Limited customization (no manual parameter tweaking)
  • Strict content policies block some creative prompts
  • No native upscaling or variation controls

Who Should Use What

Choose Midjourney if:

  • You’re a digital artist or designer wanting portfolio-worthy images
  • You enjoy tweaking parameters and experimenting with styles
  • You need high-resolution outputs for print or large displays
  • You’re part of a creative team that values unique aesthetics

Choose DALL-E 3 if:

  • You’re a marketer or content creator needing quick, accurate visuals
  • You want a simple, all-in-one solution with ChatGPT
  • You need readable text in images (e.g., infographics, ads)
  • You’re on a tight budget and want a free option

Bottom Line Verdict

Both tools have their strengths, but Midjourney remains the king of artistic quality and creative control. If you’re willing to climb the learning curve, it will reward you with images that feel like art. DALL-E 3 is the pragmatic choice—fast, accurate, and user-friendly. For most professionals who prioritize speed and simplicity, DALL-E 3 is a solid workhorse. But for those who demand the best-looking images possible, Midjourney is the winner in 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better, Midjourney or DALL-E 3?

It depends on your needs. Midjourney offers superior artistic quality and customization, while DALL-E 3 is easier to use and better at following complex prompts. For creative professionals, Midjourney wins; for marketers and beginners, DALL-E 3 is often the better choice.

Is DALL-E 3 free?

DALL-E 3 has a free tier via Bing Image Creator, but it’s slower and lower resolution. Full access requires a ChatGPT Plus subscription ($20/month).

Can Midjourney generate text in images?

Midjourney has improved but still struggles with accurate text rendering. DALL-E 3 is significantly better at generating readable text within images.

Which AI image generator is best for commercial use?

Both allow commercial use, but DALL-E 3 has clearer licensing for businesses (via OpenAI). Midjourney’s terms also permit commercial use for paid subscribers, but always check the latest policies.

Do I need Discord to use Midjourney?

Yes, Midjourney operates entirely through Discord. You must join their server and use slash commands to generate images. This can be a barrier for some users.

Which one is more affordable?

DALL-E 3 is cheaper if you already use ChatGPT Plus ($20/month for multiple tools). Midjourney’s basic plan is $10/month, but you get fewer features. For heavy users, Midjourney’s Pro plan ($60/month) can be more cost-effective per image.
